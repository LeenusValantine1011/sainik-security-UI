About US

Sainic Security Services

Protecting People and Places

At Sainic Security Services, we believe that safety is not just a service—it’s a responsibility. Established in 2014, we are one of South India's leading providers of integrated security, housekeeping, and electronic surveillance solutions, serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. With over a decade of experience, we have built a reputation for excellence by combining highly trained manpower with cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to provide clients with peace of mind, knowing their assets, spaces, and people are protected with unwavering commitment.

Who we are?

We are a solution-focused team offering complete protection services — from trained security guards to smart automation systems. With skilled personnel across security, housekeeping, and tech support, we’re committed to reliable, high-quality service. Our belief is simple: One Company. Many Solutions. One Responsibility — Your Safety.

What we do?

We provide a complete range of services including manned security, professional housekeeping, and electronic security systems like CCTV, access control, and fire safety. Beyond deployment, we design and maintain customized solutions to match each client’s unique needs.

Who We Serve?

We serve a wide range of sectors including residential communities, corporate offices, educational institutions, retail and hospitality spaces, industrial sites, and healthcare facilities, delivering trusted security and facility solutions tailored to each environment.
